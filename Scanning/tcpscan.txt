Options on nmap:

-Pn: assumes host is online and then scans
-st: Three way handshake of tcp scan
-ss: Lesser version of st
-sU: UDP scan
-sA: Only sends ACK back, so bypasses some blocled websites
-g/--source port "port": For entry though specific port
--data-length "length": Bypass map standard packet size
--spoof-mac "Mac address": Change your mac address on the go
